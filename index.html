About Us

Welcome to Nivik Hospital, Jaipur

Nivik Hospitals, Jaipur is located at Chitrakoot, Vaishali Nagar Jaipur. This Hospital spread over a plot area of 42,033 Sq. ft with built up area of 192550 Sq. ft. is one of the fully equipped multi-specialty hospitals situated in the Western part of Jaipur. It is one of its kind multi-specialty hospitals having 237 beds with 23 operational Critical Care beds for medical, surgical & neonatal care.
